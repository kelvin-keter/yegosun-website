Who We Are

Powering Africa's Future

We are more than an energy company. We are engineers, innovators, and partners in Kenya's sustainable growth.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of renewable energy in East Africa by providing affordable, reliable, and high-quality solar engineering solutions for schools, businesses, and communities.

Our Vision

To be the undisputed leader in green energy infrastructure, creating a future where every Kenyan institution has access to clean, uninterrupted power.

Our History

A Legacy of Innovation

2018

The Academic Vision

Founded during Nixon Kipkemei's studies in BSc. Energy & Environmental Technology at JKUAT. Yegosun began as a research-driven initiative to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical renewable energy solutions for rural Kenya.

2021

Engineering Pilots

Transitioned from research to implementation. Successfully commissioned our first off-grid solar pilots in Juja, proving that sustainable micro-grids were a viable, cost-effective alternative for local communities.

2026

Yegosun Energy Limited

Officially incorporated as a premier energy partner, headquartered at Garden City. Today, we lead the market in commercial and industrial solar engineering, serving clients nationwide.
